How does Betclover farming work?

Get BTC tokens

BTC can be mined by playing games on Betclover or can be purchased on one of the exchanges.

Stake BTC tokens

Place your BTC in the Farming pool to start additional earnings. Just click "Stake" and enter the required BTC amount.

Receive more BTC

Immediately after you Stake the tokens, you will receive a profit in BTC due to the current APR. Harvest or compound your BTC.
