How does GoldMineBets farming work?

Get GMB tokens

GMB can be mined by playing games on GoldMine or can be purchased on one of the exchanges.

Stake GMB tokens

Place your GMB in the Farming pool to start additional earnings. Just click "Stake" and enter the required GMB amount.

Receive more GMB

Immediately after you Stake the tokens, you will receive a profit in GMB due to the current APR. Harvest or compound your GMB.

How to get BFG tokens?

Play any games

GMB tokens can be mined by playing games on GoldMine in any available currency. With each bet, GMB tokens are automatically adding to your balance.

Buy on exchange

GMBcan be purchased on Biswap exchange. Buy and Sell GMB tokens anywhere and anytime with the lowest 0.1% fee.
