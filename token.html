BTC Staking

Staking is the system of multicurrency daily drops for all BTC token holders. Every 24 hours, the Staking pool releases 3% of the total profit of the platform in each currency. The amount is shared among all users who hold BTC tokens. The more BTC tokens, the greater Staking income! BTC token became a tradable asset! Buy and Sell BTC tokens anytime!

All tokens mined before going to exchange are available for trading. New tokens will be locked for withdrawals. The user will be able to get Staking rewards and play with BTC until the tokens are unlocked.
